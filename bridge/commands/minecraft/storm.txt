// 突風：全員が吹き上げられてから低速落下で着地（落下ダメージなし）
effect give @a minecraft:levitation 2 12 true
effect give @a minecraft:slow_falling 15 0 true

execute at @a run playsound entity.ender_dragon.flap master @a ~ ~ ~ 1.4 0.5
execute at @a run particle minecraft:cloud ~ ~2 ~ 8 4 8 0.5 300 force
